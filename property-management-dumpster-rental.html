Typical Load Weights & Bin Selection

A standard 2-bedroom apartment cleanout fills about half a 20-yard bin. A hoarder house or estate cleanout typically fills 1-2 full bins. Most tenant turnovers with some renovation (carpet, paint, minor demo) fill one bin. We can schedule recurring pickups for property management companies turning multiple units.
